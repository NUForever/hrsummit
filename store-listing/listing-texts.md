### Full description

Train smarter in the mountains. HRsummit is the heart rate monitor built for altitude.

Connect your Garmin HRM-600 or any BLE heart rate strap and get real-time performance metrics designed specifically for high-altitude mountaineering.

FREE FEATURES
- Real-time heart rate with automatic zone detection
- Basic HR zones (5-zone model)
- Sessions up to 30 minutes
- Basic HRV (RMSSD)

PREMIUM FEATURES ($4.99/month or $34.99/year)
- Unlimited session duration
- DFA alpha1 — detect your aerobic threshold in real time, even as it drops with altitude
- TRIMP — cumulative training load (Banister)
- EPOC — recovery demand estimation
- Baevsky Stress Index — autonomic balance
- LF/HF ratio — altitude acclimatization tracking
- Estimated respiratory rate
- Cardiac drift — aerobic efficiency in long sessions
- HRV trends — progressive fatigue during sessions
- Session history — review all past sessions
- Recovery score
- CSV export for post-session analysis
- VO2max estimation

WHY HRsummit FOR HIGH ALTITUDE
- DFA alpha1 automatically detects how your aerobic threshold drops with altitude
- LF/HF > 3.0 indicates incomplete acclimatization
- Estimated respiratory rate reveals hyperventilation from hypoxia
- Baevsky Index shows autonomic stress caused by altitude
- All metrics include altitude-contextualized interpretation

BUILT FOR THE OUTDOORS
- High-contrast display, readable in direct sunlight
- Foreground service: keeps monitoring with the screen off
- BLE auto-reconnect if the connection drops
- Session persistence — if the app closes, your data is recovered
- No internet required — 100% local processing

COMPATIBILITY
- Any BLE chest strap with Heart Rate Service (0x180D)
- Garmin HRM-600, HRM-Pro, HRM-Dual
- Polar H10, H9, OH1
- Wahoo TICKR, TICKR X

PRIVACY
Your health data never leaves your device. No analytics, no trackers, no external servers.

5 tabs: Live · Zones · Performance · HRV · Settings
