HRsummit — Data Deletion

Last updated: March 26, 2026

How Your Data is Stored

HRsummit stores all data locally on your device only. No health or training data is sent to any external server. Data is stored in your device's IndexedDB and localStorage.

How to Delete Your Data

Step 1: Open your device Settings

Step 2: Go to Apps > HRsummit

Step 3: Tap "Clear Data" to delete all app data, or "Uninstall" to remove the app and all its data completely

Both options will permanently delete all data stored by HRsummit on your device.

Subscription Data

If you have an active premium subscription, your subscription is managed by Google Play and RevenueCat. Deleting app data or uninstalling does not cancel your subscription. To cancel:

Go to Google Play Store > Profile > Payments & subscriptions > Subscriptions > HRsummit > Cancel
